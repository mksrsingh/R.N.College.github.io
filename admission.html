Eligibility & Selection Criteria

Students passing out senior secondary exam of Bihar School Examination Board, Patna or its equivalent exam in the current or earlier session may apply for admission to the 1st year of the 3 years Degree course. Admission to various degree courses are made strictly on the merit of the applicant in the qualifying exam (+2 or as the case may be).

Minimum Qualification :
The minimum qualification required to pursue the Graduation courses is 11th passed from a recognized board. Graduation passed students are also eligible to pursue the PG programs. The decision of the university, regarding eligibility of any applicant shall be final.

For College Admission, Students have to submit these mentioned documents along with the application form :

  • Original Mark Sheet.
  • Original School Leaving Certificate (SLC)/ College Leaving Certificate (CLC), which will be originally submitted at the college.
  • Character certificate.
  • Migration certificate (for students Passing +2 level exam from a Board other than Bihar School Exam. Board, Patna).
  • Caste and Income certificate (where ever applicable).
  • Self-addressed duly stamped envelope.
  • He/She must pay his/her admission and other requisite fee as specified in the Prospectus.
